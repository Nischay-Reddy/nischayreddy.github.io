Research

My research focuses on Algebraic Number Theory and the Langlands Program, particularly the $p$-adic aspects.

Current Projects

  • Project 1: Investigating local-global compatibility in the $p$-adic Langlands correspondence.
  • Project 2: Exploring connections between Galois representations and automorphic forms.
  • Project 3: Studying deformation theory and its applications to arithmetic geometry.

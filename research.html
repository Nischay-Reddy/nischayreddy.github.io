Research

My research is in the intersection of algebraic number theory and the $p$-adic Langlands program. I am especially interested in:

  • Deformations of Galois representations
  • Moduli of local Langlands parameters
  • Interpolation of automorphic forms

For example, I’ve been studying the geometry of certain stacks parametrizing $(\varphi, \Gamma)$-modules and their relationship to $p$-adic Hodge theory.

More details and preprints to follow!
